Flutter Bootcamp 2020

 

gesture What is this about?

 

Flutter Bootcamp is a 5 day online bootcamp hosted by Developer Student Club MBCET which will help you to get started with Flutter and build apps for multiple platforms. The program aims at beginners and doesn't expect the participants to have any prior experience in futter. We will be adding the lectures to our YouTuve Channel each day in the morning. You will have 24 hrs to follow up and implement them. The progam is free and open to all. Do sign up in order to have a seamless experience for #flutterbootcamp.

 

 

 

 

 

 

filter_drama Getting Started

 

Get introduced to Flutter, directly from Google!

Flutter is really amazing considring the fact that applications of multiple platforms can be developed using a single code base.

After completion of the bootcamp, you will have developed a variety of applications and moreover the skill required to buid any application right away. What are you waiting for? Register now itself and start learning.
